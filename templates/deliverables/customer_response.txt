Customer Response Template (Plain Text)
=======================================

Purpose: Professional customer-facing email/case update
Format: PLAIN TEXT ONLY - no markdown formatting

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                              TEMPLATE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Hello,

Thank you for your patience while we investigated [case description].

ROOT CAUSE
----------
[Explain in plain English what caused the issue. 2-3 sentences max.
No jargon, no confidence percentages, no pattern IDs.]

RESOLUTION
----------
[What Engineering/TAC is doing to fix it, OR steps customer should take]

1. [First step with specific details]
2. [Second step]
3. [Third step if needed]

NEXT STEPS
----------
[What happens next - who does what, any timeline if known]

Please let us know if you have any questions.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
Palo Alto Networks TAC

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                           GUIDELINES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DO:
- Use plain, clear language
- Be specific about resolution steps
- Acknowledge customer's patience
- Offer continued support

DON'T:
- Use markdown (##, **, -, etc.)
- Include confidence percentages
- Reference internal tools (CAPPY, pattern IDs)
- Include raw log snippets
- Make timeline promises you can't keep

TONE:
- Professional and empathetic
- Clear and concise
- Actionable

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                           EXAMPLE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Hello,

Thank you for your patience while we investigated the platform slowness
after your upgrade to XSIAM 3.3.

ROOT CAUSE
----------
The issue is backend resource contention. After the upgrade, the platform
requires more resources than currently allocated to your tenant. This is
entirely server-side - your network and configuration are working correctly.

RESOLUTION
----------
We've escalated this to Engineering to increase the backend resource
allocation for your tenant. This is the same resolution that worked for
other customers with identical symptoms.

1. Engineering will review resource allocation for your tenant
2. They will apply the resource increase (similar to previous cases)
3. We'll confirm with you once complete

NEXT STEPS
----------
No action required from you. We'll update you once Engineering confirms
the resource adjustment has been applied.

Please let us know if you have any questions or if the situation changes.

Best regards,
Kevin Tan
Palo Alto Networks TAC
